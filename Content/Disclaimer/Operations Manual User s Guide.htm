Operations Manual User’s Guide

The following guide has been developed to aid in navigation and maximize use of the Operations Manual. There are two common methods used to locate information: the Table of Contents and the Search Feature.

 

Table of Contents

The Operations Manual opens with the Table of Contents displayed on the left under the “Contents” tab. The Table of Contents is organized by chapters. The chapters were designed from a restaurant leader’s viewpoint to support ease of sourcing information including:

  • The Culver’s Story

  • Recruiting, Hiring and Retention

  • Team Training and Development

  • Guest Service and Hospitality

  • Daily Operating Procedures

  • Recipes and Product Builds

  • Marketing and Public Relations

  • Safety and Security

  • Facilities and Equipment

  • Back Office Management Systems

  • Accounting Management

  • Expansion, Construction, Renewal and Resale

  • Resource Directory

 

Chapters and topics will appear as icons.

  • Book Icon - opens like a folder to display additional topics below.

  • Page Icon - opens a topic to display in the “Active Window” to the right. The first “Page Icon” within each chapter contains commonly printed “Forms and Tools” used in restaurant.

Note: Once the topic is opened, use the “Arrows” above the “Active Window” to navigate to the next or previous topic in the Table of Contents.

 

Search Feature

The Search Feature allows you to search content of the Operations Manual with a full-text search. A full-text search examines all the words stored within the content and tries to match the text specified.

  • Search Results - The “Active Window” displays search results as a topic link. To view, click the “topic” link. Clicking the “topic” link will also open the topic within the Table of Contents. To return to the search results, click the “Browser Back” button.

  • Returns ‘0’ Results - If no results display it will be necessary to alter the search words.

  • Phrase Search - The use of quotation marks (“) may increase the precision of the search when the exact words or phase are known

Tip: Key words will improve results, particularly if the words are within the content. Commonly used words throughout the Operations Manual or words used to describe the subject of the content may not provide results with a full-text search.

 

Balance navigation by utilizing both the Search Feature as well as the Table of Contents. Keep in mind; the chapters were designed from a restaurant leader’s viewpoint to simplify the process of accessing information.

 

Search Examples

  • Concrete Blending Procedure - Returns ‘0’ result; describing subject of content rather than key words. Alter search word(s).

  • Concrete Blending - Returns all topics that contain the words Concrete or Blending.

  • “Fruit Cooler” - Returns topics containing words Fruit and Cooler next to each other in this order.

 

Links

Throughout the Operations Manual you will encounter a variety of links to aid in locating content.

  • Quick Reference Links - are available at the start of topics such as ButterBurger Builds to navigate to specific content rather than scrolling. To return to the topic, use the “Browser Back” button.

  • Equipment Owner’s Manuals - clicking an “Owner’s Manual” link will display content as a PDF in the “Active Window”. The content contains the manufacturer’s standard operating procedures. Procedures specific to Culver’s will be outlined beneath each “Owner’s Manual” link where necessary to ensure proper care and maintenance of Foodservice Equipment.

  • Web Links - online systems are accessible within the Operations Manual related to specific content such as Culver’s BBU E-Learning website.

 

Resource Directory

The Resource Directory information is listed by four topics including:

  • Blue Pages - directory of approved Supplier Partners with corresponding Culver Franchising Contact’s where appropriate; organized by category.

  • Support Team - directory of Culver Franchising Support Team Members organized alphabetically; including images for visual reference.

  • Franchise Advisory Council - directory of elected franchise partners organized by territory.

 

Printing

Content can be printed directly from the Operations Manual for use in restaurant.

  • Entire Topic - click the “Printer” icon above the “Active Window”.

  • Portion of a Topic - highlight the area and click the “Printer” icon above the “Active Window”. Check the “Selection” option under “Page Range” in the dialog box.

  • Forms and Tools - commonly printed Forms and Tools will display as “links” in the “Active Window”.

    • A link containing a Word document or an Excel spread sheet will prompt you to “Open” to view or “Save” to download. Select “Print” function under “File” in top menu bar.

    • A link containing a PDF will open in the “Active Window”. Click the “Printer” icon above the “Active Window”. To return, use the “Browser Back” button.

 

Confidentiality

To gain access to the Operations Manual you must log on to the Online Support Center. Creating a shortcut to the address is not recommended and will require entry of your username and password for security purposes.

Note: Internet Explorer is the preferred web browser.

 

Ongoing Updates

The Operations Manual is the most current source of reference information. The manual is updated monthly to reflect changes based on recent communications.

 

The Operations Manual serves as the foundation for Culver’s best practices and standard operating procedures. All manuals and guides have been consolidated as content and reside within the manual making it easier to resource information in a central location. Moving forward all guides created communicating support of key initiatives will follow the same process of consolidation.
